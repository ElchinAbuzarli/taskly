Tasks & Subtasks

Break down any project into granular tasks. Nest subtasks infinitely, assign owners, set due dates, and track priority — all in one place.

  • Inline subtask creation
  • Priority labels (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
  • Due dates & reminders
  • Task comments & attachments

Team Collaboration

Invite teammates, assign tasks, and get real-time updates. Your whole team stays in sync without the endless status meetings.

  • Real-time multiplayer editing
  • @mentions & notifications
  • Role-based access control
  • Activity feed per project

Notes & Docs

Write specs, meeting notes, and wikis alongside your tasks. Docs live in the same workspace — no context switching to another tool.
